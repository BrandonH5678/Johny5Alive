### What We Have Built

**J5A** is an overnight queue/batch management system that coordinates multiple AI subsystems with:
- Constitutional AI governance (7 ethical principles, industry-leading)
- Intelligent resource management (prevents crashes, protects hardware)
- Validation-first architecture (quality gates before resource allocation)
- Business-first design (revenue operations never interrupted)

**Squirt** is a business document automation system that:
- Processes voice memos, paper worksheets, and SMS into professional PDFs
- Generates invoices, contracts, quotes, and job reports
- Maintains <30 second document generation for WaterWizard operations
- Receives absolute priority during business hours (6am-7pm Mon-Fri)

**Sherlock** is an intelligence analysis platform that:
- Processes podcast/video content for evidence extraction
- Identifies speakers, extracts claims, detects contradictions
- Builds relationship graphs and analysis reports
- Operates during off-hours to avoid business conflicts
